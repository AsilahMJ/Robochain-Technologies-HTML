FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What is agile training? Agile training is a process of teaching individuals and teams the principles and practices of agile methodologies, such as Scrum or Kanban, to improve their ability to deliver high-quality products and services.
Why is agile training important? Agile training is important because it helps organizations and teams adapt to changing market demands, improve collaboration and communication, increase productivity, and deliver value to customers more effectively.
Who can benefit from agile training? Agile training can benefit individuals, teams, and organizations across various industries and roles, including project managers, product owners, developers, testers, and executives.
What is an agile transformation? An agile transformation is the process of adopting agile principles and practices at an organizational level. It involves a shift in mindset, culture, processes, and structures to enable agility and improve business outcomes.
How long does an agile transformation typically take? The duration of an agile transformation varies depending on the size and complexity of the organization. It can range from several months to a few years. The pace of the transformation is often tailored to the specific needs and readiness of the organization.
